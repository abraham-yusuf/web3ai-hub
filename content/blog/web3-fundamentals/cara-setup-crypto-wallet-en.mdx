# How to Set Up a Crypto Wallet: Step-by-Step

Before you can use blockchain, DeFi, or NFTs, you need a **crypto wallet**. This wallet isn't like a physical wallet that holds money — it's more like a **digital key** that gives you access to your assets on the blockchain.

## What is a Crypto Wallet?

A crypto wallet is an application that stores your **private key** — the secret password that proves ownership of your assets on the blockchain. The wallet doesn't physically hold crypto; crypto always lives on the blockchain. The wallet only stores the key to access it.

## Types of Wallets

### Hot Wallets (Online)
- **MetaMask** — Browser extension + mobile, most popular
- **Trust Wallet** — Multichain mobile wallet
- **Rainbow** — Beautiful UI, beginner-friendly
- **Phantom** — Main wallet for Solana

### Cold Wallets (Offline)
- **Ledger** — Most popular hardware wallet
- **Trezor** — Open-source hardware wallet
- **Keystone** — Air-gapped hardware wallet

**Beginner recommendation:** Start with MetaMask (free). When your assets exceed $1000, consider a hardware wallet.

## Setting Up MetaMask: Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Install MetaMask
Visit metamask.io → Download → Add to Browser

### Step 2: Create New Wallet
Click the MetaMask icon → "Create a new wallet" → Set a strong password

### Step 3: Save Your Seed Phrase (CRITICAL!)
MetaMask will show **12 words** (seed phrase). This is the **master key** to your wallet.

⚠️ **WARNING:** This seed phrase is the ONLY way to recover your wallet. If lost, your assets are gone forever. If someone else gets it, they can steal everything.

**How to store safely:**
- ✅ Write on paper, store in a safe
- ✅ Use metal backup (fireproof steel plate)
- ✅ Store in multiple separate locations
- ❌ Do NOT screenshot
- ❌ Do NOT store in cloud storage
- ❌ Do NOT share with anyone

### Step 4: Verify Seed Phrase
MetaMask will ask you to verify by ordering the 12 words correctly.

### Step 5: Wallet Ready!
You'll see your address, balance, and network (default: Ethereum Mainnet).

## Receiving Crypto
1. Click "Receive" in MetaMask
2. Copy your wallet address (or scan QR code)
3. Send this address to the sender

⚠️ Make sure the sender uses the correct network. Sending to the right address but wrong network can result in lost funds.

## Sending Crypto
1. Click "Send"
2. Paste destination address
3. Enter amount
4. Select network (make sure it's correct!)
5. Review gas fee
6. Click "Confirm"

**Tip:** Always send a small test amount first before sending large amounts.

## Wallet Security

### Do's
- ✅ Use a strong, unique password
- ✅ Store seed phrase offline
- ✅ Verify URLs before connecting wallet
- ✅ Revoke unused approvals (revoke.cash)
- ✅ Use hardware wallet for large amounts

### Don'ts
- ❌ Never share your seed phrase
- ❌ Never connect to suspicious websites
- ❌ Never click links from unknown DMs
- ❌ Never approve transactions you don't understand
- ❌ Never store seed phrase digitally

## Key Takeaways

- Crypto wallets store private keys, not crypto itself
- MetaMask is the best starting point for beginners
- Seed phrase is the master key — store safely, NEVER share
- Always verify network and address before transactions
- Upgrade to hardware wallet when assets are significant
